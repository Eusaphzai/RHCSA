**Summary**
LILO stands for [[Linux Loader]].  LILO is a Linux [[Boot Loader]] that was the most popular Linux Boot Loader before [[GRUB]] took over. LILO is still used, but it is no longer in active development.

To learn more about LILO, visit here:
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LILO_(boot_loader)
