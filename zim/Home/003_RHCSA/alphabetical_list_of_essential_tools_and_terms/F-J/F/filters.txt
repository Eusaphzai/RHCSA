**KYWRD:** Linux/Unix filters
**RHCSA Objective: **001c Use grep and regular expressions to analyze text

**Summary:**
A **filter** is a Linux/Unix utility that accepts an input on standard in (**stdin**), performs useful work, and then produces an output on standard out (**stdout**) and standard error (**stderr**). Filters can be pipelined: the output of one filter can be piped into the input of another filter. The sequences of connected filters can be used to perform very significant tasks.
