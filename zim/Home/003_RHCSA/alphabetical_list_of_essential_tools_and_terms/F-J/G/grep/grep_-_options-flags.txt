**KYWRD:** grep
**RHCSA Objective: **001c Use grep and regular expressions to analyze text

**Summary:**
This article describes the options available with the [[grep]] command.

**KEYWORD - options:**
* -c: tells grep to print the number of matched lines.
* -v: tells grep to print the lines that don't match the given pattern.
