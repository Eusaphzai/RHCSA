**KYWRD:** RHCSA

**Summary**
[[RHCSA]] is an acronym for [[Red Hat Certified System Administrator]], an IT industy certification offered by [[Red Hat]] Corporation. RHCSA is an entry-level certification that certifies that the associated RHCSA professional has demonstrated a minimum level of proficiency administering [[Red Hat Enterprise Linux]], and/or equivalent computer operating systems (e.g. [[CentOS]]). The certification exam for RHCSA is hands on and focused testing whether the prospective RHCSA has achieved the core competencies required to successfully administer, install, and configure [[Red Hat Enterprise Linux]] ([[RHEL]]).

**RHCSA:**
* RHCSA - function: Provide competent certified Linux professionals to industry.
* RHCSA - description: An entry-level IT certification that certifies that one has demonstated a minimum skill level in administering Red Hat Enterprise Linux systems.
