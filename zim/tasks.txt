**Summary**
[[RHCSA]] is an acronym for [[Red Hat Certified System Administrator]], an IT industy certification offered by [[Red Hat]] Corporation.  RHCSA is an entry-level certification that certifies that the associated RHCSA professional has demonstrated a minimum level of proficiency administering [[Red Hat Enterprise Linux]], and/or equivalent computer operating systems (e.g. [[CentOS]]).
