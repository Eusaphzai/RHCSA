====== systemctl ======
Created Thursday 19 July 2018

[[systemd]]
[[tasks template]]
[[alphabetical list of essential tools]]

**Summary**
systemctl is used to start, stop and view the status of systemd processes. To see which version of systemd is installed on your system, run this command:
systemctl --version

More information on systemctl can be found here:
* https://www.freedesktop.org/software/systemd/man/systemctl.html
* https://www.tecmint.com/manage-services-using-systemd-and-systemctl-in-linux/
