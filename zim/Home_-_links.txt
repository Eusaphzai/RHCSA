**KYWRD:** RHCSA

**Summary**
RHCSA stands for Red Hat Certified Administrator. To become a certified RHCSA, the candidate must pass a 2.5 hour hands on exam (no multiple choice) with a minimum score of 210 out of 300 points (70%).
