====== gigabyte ======
Created Monday 30 July 2018

[[df:df - examples:df - example 1]]

**Summary**
1 gigabyte (GB) of space listed in the [[du]] and [[df]] utlilities equals all of the following:
* 1 [[gigibyte]] 
* 2^30 bytes
* 1024^3 bytes
* 1,073,741,824 bytes
