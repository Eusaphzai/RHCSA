====== Phil Zimmermann ======
Created Tuesday 17 July 2018

[[OpenPGP]]

Phil Zimmermann
* born February 12, 1954
* created [[Pretty Good Privacy]] ([[PGP]]), widely used email encryption software
* follows the [[OpenPGP]] standard for encrypting/decrypting data defined in  [[RFC 4880]]

More information on Phil Zimmermann can be found here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phil_Zimmermann
