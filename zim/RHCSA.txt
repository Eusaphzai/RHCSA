**Summary**
[[Red Hat Certified System Administrator]] ([[RHCSA]]) is [[Red Hat|Red Hat's]] entry-level certification. The certification exam for RHCSA is hands on and focused testing whether the prospective RHCSA has achieved the core competencies required to successfully administer, install, and configure [[Red Hat Enterprise Linux]] ([[RHEL]]).
