[[RHCSA]]

**Summary**
This is the homepage for this [[RHCSA]] Study Guide.  This guide is designed to prepare prospective Linux system administrators for the [[exam|RHCSA exam]].

More information on the RHCSA certification can be found at these resources:
* https://www.redhat.com/en/services/certification/rhcsa
* https://www.redhat.com/en/services/training/ex200-red-hat-certified-system-administrator-rhcsa-exam
* https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Hat_Certification_Program

The [[GitHub]] repository associated with this study guide is here:
* https://github.com/RexDjere/RHCSA
