====== star -c deep dive ======
Created Saturday 14 July 2018

[[star-options]]

star-options
* -c
	* creates a new tarfile and writes named files to it
	* writing starts at the beginning of the tarfile
	* verbosity can be increased by adding the -v (verbose) option

This deep dive will take a closer look at with tarfiles with the [[star]] -c command in [[star example 1]].
